Project Overview

Power BI Modeling & Approach

  • Designed a clean star schema data model optimized for performance and scalability.
  • Built 25+ optimized DAX measuresto support KPIs, YoY growth, profitability, and trend analysis.
  • Used Power Query for data cleaning, transformation, and validation.
  • Created reusable tooltip pages for deep-dive analysis.
  • Ensured executive-friendly storytelling with minimal cognitive load and intuitive navigation.

Dashboard Highlights

  • Overview Page: Revenue, profit, YoY growth, AOV, and order volume trends.
  • AI-Style Sales Driver Tree: State ‚Üí Category ‚Üí Order Status ‚Üí Payment.
  • Interactive Tooltips: State, category, payment, and order status deep dives.
  • Operational Insights: Impact analysis of returns and cancellations on revenue and profitability.

Key Insights

  • Revenue grew from ‚Çπ6.2B ‚Üí ‚Çπ14.4Bover four years, indicating sustained business growth.
  • Electronics consistently emerged as the highest revenue-generating category.
  • Maharashtra & Karnataka are the most reliable demand centers.
  • UPI dominates digital payments, showing strong adoption and consistent growth over time.
  • Returns and cancellations reduce revenue in specific states and months.

Business Recommendations

  • Prioritise inventory and marketing for high-performing categories.
  • Strengthen fulfilment in high-return regions.
  • Optimise payment experience around UPI usage.
  • Use state-level insights for targeted growth strategies.
  • Deploy the dashboard as a weekly executive decision-support tool for leadership reviews.
